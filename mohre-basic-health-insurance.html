FAQs — AED 320 MOHRE Basic Scheme

Practical answers for HR managers and SME owners across the Northern Emirates.

Is the AED 320 scheme the cheapest legal option?

For a person aged 1–64 with no pre-existing-condition disclosure required, yes — it is the federally-set minimum compliant premium. Older employees (65+) may need an enhanced plan.

Can I refund the second-year premium?

Yes — if the work permit is cancelled in the first year, the second year of the AED 320 premium is refundable to the employer.

Does it cover dependents?

The federal basic plan can be extended to dependents at the same AED 320 rate per person. Some emirates (notably Abu Dhabi) require employer-provided dependent cover at higher minimum standards.

What is the fine for non-compliance?

AED 500 to AED 2,500 per uncovered worker per month. Repeat offences can attract up to AED 500,000 and visa blocks for the company.
