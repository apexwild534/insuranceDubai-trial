Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

Is motor insurance compulsory in UAE?

Yes. Third-party liability motor insurance is legally mandatory for all vehicles in the UAE. Driving without valid insurance is a serious offence carrying fines and vehicle impoundment.

How much is car insurance in Dubai?

Comprehensive car insurance in Dubai typically costs 2–3.5% of your vehicle's value per year. A mid-range car worth AED 80,000 would cost approximately AED 1,600–2,800/year.

What is agency repair in UAE motor insurance?

Agency repair means your damaged vehicle is repaired exclusively at the manufacturer's authorised service centre, ensuring genuine parts and trained technicians are used.

How do I claim on my car insurance in Dubai?

In the event of an accident, contact the police immediately, obtain a police report, and then notify your insurer. InsuranceDubai.com's claims team can manage the entire process on your behalf.
