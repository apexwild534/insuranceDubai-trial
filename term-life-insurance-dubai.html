Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

What is term life insurance?

Term life insurance provides a guaranteed death benefit if you die within the policy term. If you outlive the policy, no benefit is paid. It is the most affordable form of life cover.

How long should my term life insurance be in UAE?

The most common terms in Dubai are 20–30 years. A good benchmark is to cover until your youngest child is financially independent and your mortgage is repaid.

What is the maximum cover for term life insurance in UAE?

UAE insurers typically provide term life cover up to AED 10 million (approximately USD 2.7 million) for high-income earners. Sums above this require medical underwriting and financial justification.

Can I get term life insurance in UAE with pre-existing conditions?

Many insurers cover pre-existing conditions with an exclusion endorsement or a premium loading. Our advisors specialist in finding the best terms for clients with medical history.
