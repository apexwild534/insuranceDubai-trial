Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

Do UAE residents need a Schengen visa insurance?

Yes. Schengen visa applications require travel insurance with minimum EUR 30,000 medical cover. InsuranceDubai.com provides Schengen-compliant policies instantly.

Is COVID-19 covered by travel insurance in UAE?

Most current UAE travel insurance policies include COVID-19 medical expenses and some cover trip cancellation if you test positive before departure. Check your specific policy.

What is annual multi-trip travel insurance?

Annual multi-trip covers unlimited journeys within a policy year, making it cost-effective for UAE residents who travel more than 3–4 times per year.

Can I buy travel insurance after booking my trip?

Yes, you can purchase travel insurance any time before departure. However, cancellation cover only applies to events occurring after your policy start date.
