Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

How many employees do I need for group health insurance in UAE?

Most UAE insurers offer group health insurance plans from as few as 2–3 employees. Larger groups of 20+ employees access the best rates and most flexible plan designs.

Is group health insurance cheaper than individual in UAE?

Yes. Group schemes benefit from pooled risk, which typically results in lower premiums per person — often 20–40% cheaper than equivalent individual policies for the same coverage.

Can UAE small businesses get group health insurance?

Absolutely. We specialise in group health for small UAE businesses. Even a company with 3–5 employees can access competitive group health plans from major UAE insurers.

What is the network for group health insurance in Dubai?

Networks vary by insurer and plan level. Premium plans access virtually all private hospitals in Dubai including American Hospital, Mediclinic, Cleveland Clinic and Emirates Hospital.
