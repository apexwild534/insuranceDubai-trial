Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

Is mortgage insurance mandatory in UAE?

Yes, virtually all UAE mortgage lenders require borrowers to hold life insurance equal to the outstanding loan balance. The policy is typically assigned to the bank as security.

How much does mortgage insurance cost in UAE?

Mortgage protection insurance typically costs 0.1–0.3% of your loan value annually. For a AED 1 million mortgage, expect approximately AED 1,000–3,000 per year.

Can I use my own life insurance for mortgage protection in UAE?

Yes. Many homeowners assign an existing life insurance policy to their UAE bank rather than purchasing the bank's in-house policy, which can save significant cost.

What happens to my mortgage if I die in Dubai?

Without mortgage insurance, the lender can call in the outstanding loan. Your estate or family must repay the debt or face the property being repossessed. Mortgage insurance prevents this scenario.
