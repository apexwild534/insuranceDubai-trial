Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

What does professional indemnity insurance cover in UAE?

PI insurance covers the legal costs and compensation payments if a client claims you gave negligent professional advice or made an error in your professional service that caused them a financial loss.

Is professional indemnity insurance mandatory in UAE?

PI insurance is mandatory for certain regulated professions in the UAE including real estate agents (RERA), insurance brokers, healthcare providers and licensed engineers. It is strongly recommended for all professional service firms.

How much professional indemnity cover do I need in UAE?

Minimum PI requirements vary by profession. Real estate agents in Dubai typically require AED 1M+. Consultants should consider cover equal to their largest contract value.

What is the difference between PI and public liability insurance?

PI covers financial losses from professional negligence in your advice or services. Public liability covers physical injury or property damage you cause to third parties during your business activities.
