Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

What business insurance is mandatory in UAE?

Workers compensation and public liability insurance are effectively mandatory for most UAE businesses. Certain professional sectors like healthcare, legal and financial services have specific regulated requirements.

How much does business insurance cost in UAE?

A basic SME package combining property, liability and business interruption can start from AED 3,000–5,000 per year depending on your industry, turnover and risk profile.

What is a business interruption insurance in UAE?

Business interruption insurance replaces lost revenue and covers fixed costs if your UAE business is forced to close due to an insured event like fire, flood or equipment breakdown.

Do freelancers in UAE need professional indemnity insurance?

Yes. UAE freelancers providing professional advice or services — particularly in consulting, IT, design and financial services — should carry professional indemnity insurance.
