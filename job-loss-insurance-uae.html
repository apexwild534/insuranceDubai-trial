Frequently Asked Questions

Top questions UAE residents ask about job loss insurance in 2026.

Is job loss insurance UAE the same as ILOE?

No. ILOE is the mandatory MOHRE scheme. Private job loss insurance UAE sits on top of ILOE and pays the gap above the AED 10,000 / 20,000 cap, often for up to 12 months instead of 3.

Can I buy job loss insurance after I lose my job?

No. Like all redundancy products, you must buy the policy while you are employed and serve a minimum 90- to 180-day waiting period before the first claim is valid.

Will it cover my mortgage if I lose my job?

Yes — that is one of the most common reasons UAE residents buy this cover. Most plans pay benefits straight to your bank account, which you then use for EMIs, school fees and rent.

Do free-zone employees qualify?

Yes. Most private job-loss insurance plans accept DIFC, ADGM, JAFZA, DMCC and other free-zone employees — including those whose employer is not registered with MOHRE.
