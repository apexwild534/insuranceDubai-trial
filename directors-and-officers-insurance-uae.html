Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

What is D&O insurance in UAE?

Directors and officers insurance protects the personal assets of UAE company directors and senior managers if they are sued personally for alleged wrongful acts committed in their management capacity.

Who can sue a company director in UAE?

Directors can be sued by shareholders, creditors, employees, regulatory bodies, competitors and even the company itself for alleged breaches of duty, negligence, misrepresentation or wrongful acts.

Is D&O insurance required for DIFC and ADGM companies?

DIFC and ADGM companies are strongly advised to carry D&O insurance. Some regulated activities within these financial free zones have specific governance and insurance requirements.

How much D&O insurance does a UAE company need?

UAE SMEs typically start with AED 5M–10M cover. Larger corporations, listed companies and those with US investors or customers typically require significantly higher limits.
