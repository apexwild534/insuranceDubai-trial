Ensure your spouse and children can maintain their lifestyle, meet school fees and cover living costs if you pass away unexpectedly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

What is the best life insurance in Dubai?

The best life insurance depends on your situation. Term life is the most affordable for pure protection, while whole-of-life and investment-linked plans suit wealth-building goals. Our advisors compare all options to find your best fit.

How much does life insurance cost in Dubai?

Term life insurance starts from approximately AED 40–80 per month for a healthy non-smoker aged 30. Premiums increase with age and the amount of coverage. Use our free quote tool for a personalised estimate.

Is life insurance payout tax-free in UAE?

Yes. UAE has no income tax, so life insurance payouts to beneficiaries are completely tax-free, making life insurance an excellent financial planning tool for UAE residents.

Can I get life insurance without a medical exam in Dubai?

Some insurers offer non-medically underwritten policies up to certain coverage limits. For larger sums assured, a medical questionnaire or examination is typically required.
