Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

Is public liability insurance mandatory in UAE?

Public liability insurance is not always legally mandated, but is effectively required for most UAE business activities, including events, construction, retail and hospitality.

How much public liability insurance do I need in UAE?

UAE contracts typically require AED 5M–AED 20M of public liability cover. The amount depends on your industry, contract requirements and the scale of your operations.

What does product liability insurance cover in UAE?

Product liability insurance covers your legal liability if a product you manufacture, supply or distribute causes bodily injury or property damage to a third party.

Is employer liability different from workers compensation in UAE?

They serve similar purposes but are structured differently. Workers compensation is UAE Labour Law compliant cover for employee injuries. Employer liability covers legal claims brought by employees.
