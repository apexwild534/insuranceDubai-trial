Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

What is Institute Cargo Clauses A?

ICC A is the broadest form of marine cargo insurance, covering all risks of loss or damage to goods in transit except for specific exclusions. It is recommended for high-value shipments.

Does marine insurance cover air freight in UAE?

Yes. Marine cargo insurance extends to cover goods transported by air, road and multimodal transit as well as sea freight.

What is an open cover marine policy?

An open cover policy automatically insures all shipments within agreed parameters without needing to declare each one individually — ideal for UAE businesses with regular import/export activity.

Is marine cargo insurance mandatory in UAE?

While not legally mandatory, marine insurance is typically required by UAE banks financing trade transactions and is essential protection for any business shipping valuable goods.
