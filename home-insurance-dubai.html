Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about this insurance type in Dubai and UAE.

Is home insurance mandatory in Dubai?

Home insurance is not legally mandatory for all Dubai residents, but most mortgage lenders require buildings insurance. It is strongly recommended for both owners and tenants.

What is the difference between buildings and contents insurance?

Buildings insurance covers the physical structure of your home. Contents insurance covers your personal belongings inside. Both can be purchased together as a combined home insurance policy.

Does home insurance cover flooding in Dubai?

Standard UAE home insurance typically covers sudden water damage from burst pipes. Flooding from storms may require a specific endorsement. Recent Dubai flood events have highlighted the importance of comprehensive cover.

Can tenants get home insurance in Dubai?

Yes. Tenants can purchase contents insurance to protect their personal belongings. Buildings insurance is typically the landlord's responsibility.
