Our Story

15 years serving Dubai's insurance needs with integrity and expertise.

InsuranceDubai.com was founded in 2009 by a team of experienced insurance professionals who recognised that UAE residents deserved better access to quality insurance products and honest, independent advice. At the time, most Dubai residents were either buying insurance directly from insurers without truly understanding their options, or relying on tied agents who could only offer one company's products.

We built InsuranceDubai.com as a true independent aggregator — with no ties to any single insurer or broker. Our platform collects your details, compares options across 27+ insurers, and forwards your enquiry to the most suitable licensed UAE broker who will contact you directly. Over 15 years, we have helped over 20,000 clients across the UAE get connected to the right insurance partner.

Today, we cover every category of insurance — personal, family and business — and our panel includes 50+ UAE and international insurers. We are regulated by the Central Bank of UAE and committed to the highest standards of professional conduct.
